Think of what happened Tuesday, which we’ll break down below, as a practice round for the actual trial part of the Senate trial. Opening arguments didn’t start. Instead, we got a lengthy debate about the rules for how the Senate trial will work, with Senate Democrats introducing a number of amendments to call witnesses and documents at the outset of the trial. Senate Republicans have voted each one down, and they eventually approved rules for how the trial will work after the debate over amendments ended.
